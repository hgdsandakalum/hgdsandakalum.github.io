Research Overview

Our research focuses on developing a mobile application, "Retina-Care," that provides personalized dietary and exercise recommendations for diabetic retinopathy patients using Q-learning, a method of reinforcement learning.

  • Personalized recommendations
  • Q-learning algorithm
  • Mobile application development

Q-learning Algorithm

Implementation of Q-learning for personalized recommendations

Expert System Integration

Combining reinforcement learning with medical expertise

Mobile Application

Development of the Retina-Care app for easy patient access

Research Results

Our simulations and user testing indicate that the proposed strategy was successful in providing recommendations suitable to the unique medical conditions and preferences of diabetic retinopathy patients.
